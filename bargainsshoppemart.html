Payment Support FAQ

Why was my payment declined?

Common reasons include insufficient funds, expired card, incorrect details, or security restrictions. Contact your bank or try another payment method.

How long does payment processing take?

M-Pesa payments are usually instant. Card payments take 1-2 minutes. Bank transfers may take 1-2 business days.

Can I get a refund for payment issues?

Yes, if your payment failed but was charged, we'll process a refund within 5-7 business days after verification.
